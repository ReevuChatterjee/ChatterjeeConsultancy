About

Who we are & our approach

Chatterjee Consultancy is a boutique architecture practice based in Durgapur, West Bengal. We specialize in residential, commercial, urban, and landscape projects, delivering innovative and functional designs. In addition to architectural design, we assist clients with documentation, approvals, and file processing through government offices, ensuring smooth project execution from concept to completion. Our process emphasizes collaboration, site-driven analysis, and meticulous detailing, so that every project is not only visually striking but also practical and fully buildable.
